About SGS Attendance Portal

The SGS Attendance Portal is a modern, user-friendly system designed to streamline attendance tracking for South Gloucestershire Stroud College. This platform was created as part of a work placement project to improve the efficiency and accuracy of attendance management.

Our Mission

We aim to provide a seamless experience for students, teachers, and administrators by offering a centralized platform for attendance tracking, reporting, and analysis.

Key Features

Easy Check-In: Students can quickly submit their attendance status through an intuitive interface.
Real-Time Reports: Teachers and admins can view attendance reports instantly.
Secure Access: Role-based authentication ensures data security and privacy.
Detailed Analytics: Track attendance patterns and generate comprehensive reports.

Why Choose Us?

Built with cutting-edge technology and student feedback, our portal simplifies attendance management while maintaining the highest standards of security and accuracy.

Help

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the SGS Attendance Portal below. If you don't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

How do I submit my attendance?

Log in with your student credentials, select your class from the dashboard, and click the "Submit Attendance" button. Confirm your status (Present/Absent) and add any notes if necessary.

What should I do if I forgot my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page and follow the instructions. An email with password reset instructions will be sent to your registered email address within a few minutes.

Can I change my submitted attendance?

Once submitted, attendance records cannot be modified directly through the portal. Contact your teacher or the support team if you need to correct an entry.

How can teachers view attendance reports?

Teachers can access the attendance summary from their dashboard. Select a class and date range to view detailed attendance records and generate reports.

What does the attendance status mean?

Present: You attended the class. Absent: You were not present for the class. These statuses are recorded with the exact timestamp of submission.

Is my data secure?

Yes! The SGS Attendance Portal uses industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your personal data. Your attendance records are confidential and accessible only to authorized users.

Can I add notes with my attendance submission?

Yes, you can add optional notes when submitting your attendance. This is useful for explaining absences or providing additional context about your attendance status.

What if I encounter a technical issue?

If you experience technical problems, try refreshing your browser or clearing your cache. If the issue persists, contact the support team with details about the problem and any error messages.

Still Need Help?

If your question isn't answered here, don't hesitate to reach out to us via email at support@sgsattendance.edu or call us during business hours. Our support team is always ready to assist you!
